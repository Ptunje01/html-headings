Welcome to HTML Headings

In HTML, headings are used to give titles or subtitles to content. They go from h1 (largest) to h6 (smallest).

This is Heading 1

We use Heading 1 for the most important title of the page.

This is Heading 2

Heading 2 is usually used for section titles.

This is Heading 3

Heading 3 can be used for sub-sections under Heading 2.

This is Heading 4

Heading 4 is smaller and often used for detailed sub-sections.

This is Heading 5

Heading 5 is rarely used but can be helpful for small sections.

This is Heading 6

Heading 6 is the smallest heading and used for the least important text headings.
